Frequently Asked Questions

Have questions about selling your manga collection? Find answers below

What types of manga do you buy?

We buy all types of manga, from single volumes to complete sets. We're interested in Japanese imports, English translations, and various genres. We're particularly interested in rare, out-of-print, or limited edition manga, but we buy common titles as well.

How do you determine the value of my collection?

We evaluate manga based on several factors, including:

  • Condition (new, like new, good, acceptable)
  • Rarity and out-of-print status
  • Completeness of series
  • Current market demand
  • Special editions or variants
We always aim to offer fair market value for all collections.

How will I receive payment?

We offer several payment options:

  • PayPal (fastest option)
  • Direct bank deposit
  • Check by mail
  • Store credit (with a 10% bonus)
Most payments are processed within 48 hours of us receiving and verifying your collection.

Do you cover shipping costs?

Yes, we cover shipping costs for collections valued over $100. For smaller collections, we can deduct the shipping cost from your payment or you can arrange shipping yourself.

What if I don't accept your offer?

There's no obligation to accept our offer. If you decide not to sell to us, we can return your collection at our expense (for collections valued over $200) or you can choose to donate it.
